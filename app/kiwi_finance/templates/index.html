Why Kiwi Finance is different
We built this because we were frustrated with every other finance app. Here's what we do differently.
Your data. Your control.
We never sell your data. We don't show you ads. We don't push credit card offers. Your financial data is stored locally in SQLite and optionally exported to your own S3 bucket. You own it, you control it, you can delete it anytime.
Unlike: Mint, Credit Karma (ad-supported models)
Reliable sync via Plaid
We use Plaid's production API with incremental cursor-based syncing. Your credentials never touch our servers — Plaid handles authentication. Transactions sync daily with added, modified, and removed records tracked automatically.
Unlike: Apps with frequent disconnection issues
Flexible, not rigid
Build your own budget categories. Create custom dashboard views. Export raw data and run your own queries. We give you powerful defaults but never lock you into a single framework.
Unlike: YNAB (strict envelope budgeting)
Fast & lightweight
Local SQLite database means instant queries. No server round-trips for every chart. The dashboard loads in milliseconds, not seconds. Clean UI with no clutter, no upsells, no distractions.
Unlike: Slow, bloated web apps
Built for power users
Direct SQL access to your data. S3 exports for Athena/Redshift. Open source codebase you can fork and customize. API endpoints for building your own tools on top.
Unlike: Closed platforms with limited export
100% free, forever
No premium tiers. No feature paywalls. No "upgrade to see more than 3 months of data." Everything is free because we're not trying to monetize your financial data.
Unlike: Personal Capital, Quicken (subscription models)
